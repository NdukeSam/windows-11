Easier To Use

Explore the new Windows 11 features that help you get things done in a fast, fun and easy way.

Universal Search

Select Search on your taskbar and simply start typing what you’d like to find. You’ll get personalised results from your files, photos, contacts, emails, and even the web.

Navigate using voice recognition 1, see what's being said with live captions, or have on-screen text read aloud by a narrator in a natural, human voice. 2 Windows 11 makes it easy for everyone to use their PC.

Personalise your desktop theme, backgrounds, colours, text, and more to build the perfect creative space to explore your passions.
